Ashanty Lemus Video Art

Happiness is a warm puppy. -Snoopy


This video outlines my techniques for manipulating light and the environment to enhance the eerie atmosphere of the video using Adobe After Effects. I had one goal in mind: to change the lighting in order to create a more unsettling mood. Initially, I wasn't sure how to achieve this, so I started using the search bar in Adobe After Effects and experimented with various effects until I found one that worked well. And I was pleased with the results!

In this second video, I focused on manipulating two different code elements—parametric equations and snowflakes—from a website called 'p5.js,' which was recommended to me by two of my teachers. Originally, this was meant to be an assignment for my class; however, because I found it visually appealing, I decided to use it for this project. Fortunately, coding it didn't take long. I simply combined the two codes and created an 'if-else' statement to synchronize their tones after a few seconds.
