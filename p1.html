UFOs Take Over

I will showcase how I incorporated basic vector behavior, along with the use of transform, rotate, and scale functions. Additionally, I created two custom functions, one with arguments and one without, and implemented a 'child'/'parent' function for my UFOs and environment.
Starting with the UFOs, I used a child function to assemble the three lights, the roof, and the engine at the bottom. After assembling these components, I programmed different animations for them. One UFO moves side to side while the other bounces around. The sun also has an animation where it rotates, and the clouds move in one direction, disappearing off one side and reappearing on the other.
